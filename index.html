The Cupcake Template

The Cupcake Template is a free general-purpose single-page template by Seedersoft.

We particulary recommend this template for use on websites in the food / drink industry. With its clean looks and 100% responsive layouts, The Cupcake Template offers you a quick way to get your website online.

Powered by Bootstrap v4, jQuery, FontAwesome, MixItUp and Autosize. The stunning photographs are from Pexels. The name of the cupcakes are from here.
